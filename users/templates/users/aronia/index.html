Client’s questions

We are Very Happy to Answer Your Questions.

How can I book a trip with Aronia Travel Ltd?

You can book a trip by visiting our website and selecting your desired travel package. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team via phone or email for personalized assistance with your booking.

Can you help with visa applications?

Yes, we provide visa consultation services to assist you with your visa application process. Our team will guide you through the necessary steps and documentation required for your destination.

Are there special packages for corporate clients?

Absolutely! We offer tailored travel solutions for corporate clients, including business travel arrangements, conference and meeting planning, and more. Please contact our corporate travel team for more details.

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

Once you complete your booking, you will receive a confirmation email with all the details of your trip. If you do not receive this email, please contact our customer service team for assistance.
